ABOUT US


Daily Provisions is your neighborhood cafe with everything you need throughout the day to dine in or take away.

We open early in the morning with fresh baked goods and breakfast sandwiches. Throughout the afternoon and evening, we offer made-to-order sandwiches, slow-roasted rotisserie chicken, seasonal sides and a selection of beer and wine to toast the day.

We're an eatery from Danny Meyer's Union Square Hospitality Group, with a culinary team led by Chef Carmen Quagliata of Union Square Cafe and Pastry Chef Daniel Alvarez.
